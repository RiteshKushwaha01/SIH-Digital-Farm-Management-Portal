Risk Assessment FAQ

How often should I reassess?

Every 2 weeks, or after any major change in stock or procedures.

What increases risk most?

High density, poor hygiene, outdated vaccinations, and unrestricted access.

What should I fix first?

Address vaccination gaps and hygiene practices before other improvements.
